# Growth Intelligence Workflows

Use this module for evidence-backed market, search, competitor, content, channel, lifecycle, measurement, reporting, and app-store strategy. Route as `growth` through `growth-strategist` with `A3`.

## Evidence Rules

- Label observations by source and date; separate evidence from assumptions and recommendations.
- Do not invent search volume, rankings, CPC, traffic, backlinks, authority, conversions, churn, revenue, app-store results, campaign performance, or analytics data.
- Use current source research when a request depends on current markets, SERPs, competitors, pricing, listings, or platform behavior.
- Keep strategy separate from site implementation; route approved website changes as `web-build`.

## Research Modules

### Market, Keyword, And SERP

- Clarify market, ICP, geography, offer, funnel stage, and search intent.
- Segment keyword opportunities by intent, page type, audience, and stage.
- Inspect observed result formats before proposing content formats.
- Distinguish classic search observations from AI/answer-engine observations.

### Competitors, Backlinks, And Entities

- Separate direct, search, content, and substitute competitors.
- Require sourced evidence for backlink, authority, traffic, rank, or trust claims.
- Report positioning, entity consistency, citation, and public trust gaps conservatively.

### Content Gaps And Strategy

- Separate keyword, topic, proof, format, and funnel gaps.
- Prioritize by product fit, demand evidence, difficulty evidence, value, effort, and time-to-signal.
- Hand implementation-ready content work to `web-build`.

### Channel, Lifecycle, Launch, And Retention

- Define audience, offer, conversion event, budget, timeline, constraints, and tracking before recommendations.
- Treat ads, email, launch, churn, and retention plans as proposed strategies unless live operation is verified.
- Use ethical persuasion; never assert unsupported benchmark performance.

### Measurement, Monitoring, And Reporting

- Define event names, properties, conversion definitions, UTM rules, owners, thresholds, and reporting cadence.
- Label every metric with source, period, comparison period, and freshness.
- Distinguish measurement setup recommendations from observed dashboard evidence.

### App Store And Marketing Psychology

- Keep iOS and Google Play constraints distinct and keep ASO separate from web SEO unless a landing page is involved.
- Review listing fields, media, reviews, localization, categories, positioning, and experiments using available evidence.
- Turn messaging or behavioral ideas into ethical, testable hypotheses rather than unsupported claims.

## Output Contract

Report objective, inputs and sources, assumptions, findings, confidence, prioritized recommendations, implementation handoff, and measurement or external validation needed. Use `P0` for urgent verified risk, `P1` for high-confidence opportunity, `P2` for moderate opportunity, and `P3` for exploratory work needing more data.
